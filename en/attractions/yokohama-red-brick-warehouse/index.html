A Walk Through History and Culture

The Red Brick Warehouse was originally constructed between 1911 and 1913 to handle the bustling port trade of Yokohama. It features classic red brick architecture, which gives it a timeless, industrial aesthetic. Now, visitors can explore the site and experience a blend of the past and present. The two main warehouses (Warehouse No. 1 and Warehouse No. 2) have been carefully preserved to retain their historical charm, while modern renovations have made it a great space for cultural exhibitions and events.

Shopping, Dining, and Cultural Events
