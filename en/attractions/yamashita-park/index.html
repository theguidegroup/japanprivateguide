A Beautiful Seaside Stroll

Yamashita Park is a popular spot for both locals and tourists, featuring well-maintained gardens, fountains, and pathways lined with flowers. The park stretches along the waterfront, providing an ideal location for a relaxing walk with views of the bay and nearby city skyline.

The Hikawa Maru Ship

A major attraction at Yamashita Park is the historic Hikawa Maru, a retired ocean liner turned museum. The ship offers visitors a glimpse into the golden age of ocean travel, with its restored interiors and exhibits showcasing its long history. The Hikawa Maru is docked at the park and is a perfect addition to your visit.

Why Yamashita Park is a Must-Visit for Yokohama Travelers

Whether you're looking to relax by the water, explore history aboard the Hikawa Maru, or take in the stunning views of Yokohama Bay, Yamashita Park is a perfect destination. It offers a peaceful escape in the heart of Yokohama, and is a great way to experience the city's maritime charm.
