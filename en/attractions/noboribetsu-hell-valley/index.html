Noboribetsu Hell Valley (Jigokudani) is one of Japan's most active geothermal areas, located in Hokkaido. Known for its bubbling hot springs, steam vents, and sulfuric fumes, the valley offers visitors a truly otherworldly experience. A guided tour of Hell Valley provides valuable insights into the region's volcanic activity and unique geology.

Why You Need a Guide

While the Hell Valley is easy to explore on your own, a certified guide will enhance your visit by explaining the geological processes behind the hot springs and volcanic features. Your guide will also provide safety tips, as the area can be dangerous due to the high temperatures and active geothermal features.
