The Ryukyu Kingdom Legacy

Shurijo Castle was the political and cultural heart of the Ryukyu Kingdom for over 400 years. Though much of the original structure was destroyed in a 2019 fire, restoration efforts are underway to bring the castle back to its former glory. Visitors can explore the grounds, experience the rich history, and admire the beauty of the reconstructed buildings.

What to Expect

  • Wander through the reconstructed gardens and enjoy the panoramic views of Naha city.
