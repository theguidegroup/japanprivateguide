Take a Scenic Stroll Along the Canal

Walking along Otaru Canal is like stepping back in time. The canal is lined with old stone warehouses, some of which have been converted into museums, cafes, and shops. The reflections of the buildings in the canal make for a perfect photo opportunity, especially during sunset when the warm light creates a magical atmosphere.

Historic Warehouses and Shops

The canal area is home to several historic warehouses, many of which date back to the early 20th century. These warehouses have been preserved and repurposed for modern use, housing a variety of shops, museums, and restaurants. Stroll around the area to explore the local culture and pick up unique souvenirs like Otaru‚Äôs famous glassware and music boxes.
