Before You Book

Please check these key details before your tour.

🌸 Cancellation & Refund Policy

  • Full refund minus a 6% processing fee if the booking is canceled at least 48 hours before the scheduled tour start time.
  • 50% refund for cancellations made 24–48 hours before the tour.
  • No refund for cancellations or no-shows within 24 hours of the tour.

🌸 Meeting Point & Punctuality

  • Please arrive at the agreed meeting point 10 minutes early.
  • Tours will depart no later than 15 minutes after the scheduled time.
  • Arrivals more than 15 minutes late will be considered no-shows.

🌸 Payment & Guide Service

  • A 50% deposit is required to confirm your booking. This is paid online at the time of reservation.
  • The remaining 50% of the tour fee is payable directly to your assigned guide on the day of the tour.
  • All guiding services are independently delivered and the guide responsible for your experience.

🌸 Tour Start Time & Billing

  • Tour time begins at the scheduled meeting time, not upon your arrival.
  • Late arrivals do not affect the billing period.

🌸 Overtime & Additional Charges

  • Overtime will be charged according to the "Overtime Add-on" listed in the package.
  • Any request to extend the tour must be confirmed with the guide in advance.

🌸 What’s Included

  • The tour fee includes guide services and group coordination support.
  • Transportation, meals, and attraction tickets are not included unless explicitly stated.

🌸 Weather & Emergencies

  • Tours operate in all regular weather conditions, including light to moderate rain
  • In case of severe weather or official warnings (e.g. typhoons or storm alerts), we may reschedule or issue a full refund.

🌸 Invoice Notice

  • This invoice covers the reservation handling fee (50%) only.
  • The remaining 50% is paid directly to our guide, who can issue a separate invoice if needed.
  • Please contact us if you require assistance with invoicing.
